About

I'm a developer, I especially enjoy making casual mobile games. I know ActionScript3, Java (Android), Javascript and Unity. I live in Prague, Czech Republic and I just finished my Master's degree in Computer Science at Czech Technical University. I also spent a semester at University of Wisconsin in Madison. I am available for contract work in mobile and web games.
Don't hesitate to contact me.

Projects

Mozkovna

2014 - now
Casual mobile quiz game with a turn-base mutliplayer for Android and iOS. More than 900 000 downloads in the Czech Republic. I was the creator, mobile developer and manager. Won the Mobile App of the Year 2016 in games category.
Tech: Adobe AIR, Starling, Flox, ANE for in-app purchases, notifications...
Web

Kozel Ovozel

2016
Virtual garden, where kids can grow and harvest fruit learning new things about nature. In a fun minigame, they deliver it to their school to climb the leaderboard. Contract work, I was the mobile developer.
